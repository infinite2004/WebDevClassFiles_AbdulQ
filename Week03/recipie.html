This simple pancake recipe makes soft, fluffy pancakes with a golden-brown finish. Made from basic ingredients like flour, milk, and eggs, it’s quick to whip up and perfect for any breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons (15g) baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon (12g) granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons (45g) unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and sugar.
  2. Combine Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Mix Wet and Dry: Stir wet ingredients into dry ingredients. Do not overmix.
  4. Preheat Pan: Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  5. Cook Pancakes: Pour ¼ cup of batter onto the skillet, cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  6. Serve: Stack pancakes and serve with syrup, butter, or fruit.
